An adventure for men who seek justice for the oppressed. This is an inaugural GO Nepal Trip, where a group of men will be trekking in the Himalayas and supporting our new partner in Nepal. This organization works in rural village communities that suffer from poverty and exploitation, where many girls fall victim to human trafficking. We will support their work, which enables these villages to achieve a better future and transform the region. The community projects vary based on the needs of the villages at the time. In the past teams have helped build fish farms, taught english classes, and supported local businesses. This trip will support the ultimate mission to eliminate human trafficking in these highly vulnerable areas.

Special Considerations:

  • This trip will include moderately strenuous hiking at altitudes up to 15,000 ft (roughly half the oxygen where most of us live).
  • To be eligible for this trip, you will need to provide clearance from your medical provider, indicating your body can handle trekking 4-10 miles per day with altitude changes of 2000-4000 ft per day.
  • This trip will include some mandatory training to prepare for the physical exertion.
