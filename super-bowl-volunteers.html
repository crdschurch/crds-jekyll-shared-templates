We’re expecting 50,000 friends on Super Bowl weekend.
Want to help with the biggest party in town?

SERVE IN KIDS CLUB

Help make room for new kids by greeting new families, helping toddlers acclimate to a new environment or leading a small group in an activity that helps them discover who God is. Don’t worry, we’ll pair you with an experienced Kids’ Club volunteer who will show you around.

SERVE IN FIRST IMPRESSIONS

We love making sure that everyone has a great first impression of Crossroads. Serve by helping us get cars parked, greet folks at the doors or guide people to their seats.
