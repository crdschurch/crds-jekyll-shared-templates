Remember the Real Encounters series? Did it put a new spark in your heart for travel and adventure? If so, Encounter Israel might be for you.

The Encounter Israel trips are a journey to "ground zero" of the Bible. Trip-goers will visit Bethlehem, the Jordan River, the Sea of Galilee and Jerusalem, as well as historic Old Testament locations from the stories of Abraham, Moses, Elijah and David. Participants will see and be able to contextualize the stories from the Bible. Daily teachings will challenge you to examine and grow in your own spirituality.

It's important to understand that Encounter Israel is not meant to be a vacation or a fun tour trip. It's an opportunity to push your spiritual boundaries. It will be an investment of your time, your money, and your energy.

This is an opportunity to join other Crossroads team members in a different kind of spiritual journey. In addition, you’ll engage in the following:

  • Reflect on the teachings of the Bible and the life of Jesus
  • Worship God in his own backyard
  • Seek and receive healing
  • Grow in community with others who are serious about their spiritual growth
  • Set off on a new spiritual trajectory in your life

Finally, you can be certain of one key outcome of Encounter Israel. God will be asking, challenging, and expecting you to take your life in a bold new direction. You will be challenged to live with intentionality as a committed disciple of Jesus.
