huddle

Be immersed in content to become disciple-making disciples.

Huddle immerses 4-6 participants in the core characteristics and competencies of Jesus by giving them in-depth access to a leader who is living it out — so that after a year of training, those participants can lead others through the same process.

Get huddle leader training Get connected to a huddle Test out a huddle

Leading a Group already?
Here’s how huddle fits in.

Think of huddle as a leadership development tool within Groups. Leaders can use huddle a couple of ways.

1. You have a group of people who are following and could move towards producing. Invite them all to do huddle.

2. As your group grows, identify those who are furthest along (i.e. following). Invite the 4-6 that you want to invest in as future leaders into huddle.

Instead of huddle being a different thing altogether, it’s how you integrate discipleship in your group, multiplying mature leaders to lead more groups who’ll do the same.
