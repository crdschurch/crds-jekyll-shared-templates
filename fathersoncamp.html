Common questions

This sounds perfect. Where can I drop my son off?
Great question! To be clear: This is not a “drop your son off” camp, nor is it a day-time camp. It is an overnight camp experience.

How old does my son need to be?
Your son has to be between the ages of 7-17.

How in shape does the father need to be?
Fathers need to be able to run 1 mile in under 6 minutes. Complete 10 pull-ups and 100 crunches in under a minute; as well as have a good sense of humor.

How much does FSC cost?
$139.98 (for the both of you).

Can I take all of my sons?
Yes. One per camp. FSC is designed for one on one time with you and your son.

Can a father figure take my son?
Yes. ABSOLUTELY! There will be additional documents to sign for both the parents and or legal guardian/father figure.
