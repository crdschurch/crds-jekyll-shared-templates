huddle

It’s a group of 4-6 people training to become disciple-making disciples.

Huddles immerse participants in the core characteristics and competencies of Jesus by giving them in-depth access to a leader who is living it out — so that after a year of training, those participants can lead others through the same process.

Leading a Group already?
See how huddle fits in.

1. You have a group of people who are following and could move towards producing.

2. As your group grows, pull those who are furthest along (following) that you want to invest in as future leaders.

Instead of huddle being a different thing altogether, it’s how you integrate discipleship in your group, multiplying mature leaders to lead more groups who’ll do the same.

Starting a huddle?
You'll need training.

It’s like the right of passage to becoming a huddle leader. Part foundation check, part practice, and a whole lot of encouragement so you’ll be ready to go.

huddle taster

It's is a one-week preview of each huddle interval so you can get a feel for what huddles are like. Sign up for topics a la carte or do them all together. Your next step after a Taster is to be discipled in huddle so you can then lead others in the content.
(It’s not a shortcut for the real thing.)
