Finding Margin: Money

Tips for managing your money.

Having a plan is important. We plan parties, plan weddings, plan vacations—we even plan how we're going to clean the house! Yet 68% of Americans don't make a plan for their money. We believe that God wants us to have financial margin, but that margin can't happen when we don't know what's actually happening with our money. Here are some tools to help you get a handle on the moolah.
