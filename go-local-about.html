  • One day. 500 projects. 10,000 volunteers.
    On Saturday, May 20, we’ll tackle more than 500 projects across the country. From serving the homeless and painting schools to praying with the broken and cleaning up neighborhoods—we’ll make a difference for hundreds of deserving communities and organizations.
    Every project has a leader. One project just needs you.

  • Eleven Years. A lot of love.
    God tells us to serve those in need. To love them, learn from them, and fight for their freedom. Every May for the last eleven years, we’ve mobilized thousands of people to respond to God’s call.
    Through GO Cincinnati and Serve the City, we’ve loved hundreds of schools, churches, neighborhoods, social agencies, and nonprofits to put our faith in action and to say thank you for their tireless work.

  • Join a Project
    Serving on GO Day is easy.

1. Choose your city.
2. We match you to existing projects. Based on need, some projects fill up fast, so sign up now.
3. On GO Day, show up at the place your project leader indicates, get pumped, and get ready to work.

In 2017, we need 10,000 volunteers. So invite your friends and neighbors to serve with you. They’re gonna love it.
