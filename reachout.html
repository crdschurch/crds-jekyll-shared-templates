Featured GO Trip: India May 2019

Take a deep dive into the justice work of Mumbai—one of the largest cities in India where sexual trafficking and exploitation is rampant. But the front lines of this fight are strong. Men and women work tirelessly throughout the year to end the tragedy and bring love and care to women and children coming out on the other side. You’ll be on the relief team.
