M*A*S*H is just a group of normal dudes who want to serve one another in ministry. M*A*S*H is a “Mobile Army Surgical Hospital” that serves the men who participate at Man Camp.

Sacrifice with a purpose:

If you are a praying man and want to join the brotherhood of bringing men to freedom by pointing them to Jesus join us at Man Camp. If you’ve never served before no problem we’ll train and equip you. It’s super simple.

Common Quetions

What if I don’t know anybody going?
Perfect! You’ll make friends in no time. Every MASHer will be assigned a squad: a group of other guys, led by a Gold Leader. You’ll do everything with your squad: minister together, get outfitted, bring food, set up camp. You are going to be good friends with your squad by the time this trip is over.

What if I don't go to Crossroads?
Cool! You'll still love it. Tons of other guys who don't call Crossroads home will be serving in the M*A*S*H tent. MAN CAMP is powered by Crossroads but the movement is way bigger than that.

What if I live far away?
We've had men fly in from all over the country for Man Camp. You can too. Hear what Matt Welty has to say about Man Camp.
