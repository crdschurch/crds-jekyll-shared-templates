Host Resources

We're here to help

Thanks for stepping up to be a part of Crossroads Anywhere. We don't think you should do this alone. We've got resources to help get you energized, equipped, and excited. You're a part of the team and we can't do this without you.


1. Join a learning community

Staff and volunteers do weekly calls to equip you, ensuring your gathering is a success. Get insider tips and spiritual backup from a trainer and other peers from across the country. If that's something you want (and we think it is) email us. anywhere@crossroads.net

2. Check out our leadership video series.

If you want to be challenged and grow as a leader, this is for you. You can go at your own pace and start applying what you learn. Begin your training today.

3. Join our Facebook group for hosts.

Meet peers. Share stories. Challenge each other. (There's nothing wrong with a little healthy competition.) This is the place to meet other people doing the same thing as you! Click here to check it out.
