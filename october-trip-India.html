Kolkata Men's Trip: An adventure for men who seek justice for the poor and oppressed. You will visit & support freedom businesses located in the heart of the red light district, who provide viable employment to survivors of human trafficking. The work varies depending on the needs of our partners, but generally involves a lot of sweat, tears, laughter and seeing the amazing story of redemption happening through our partners. Half the time will be manual labor (all skill levels needed), the other half building into boys at Premenand Home.
