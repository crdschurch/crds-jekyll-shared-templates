Make plans this Thanksgiving to spend it giving back. Be part of our inaugural GO trip to Durban, South Africa to work with our partner, Build The Future. Be the first to experience the warm waters of the Indian Ocean, the beautiful countryside of KwaZulu Natal and work at the Build The Future pre schools in Ndwedwe. These are two of the newer preschools that need infrastructure work - like painting murals on containers and walls, planting food gardens to help with the feeding, putting up fencing or playground equipment. There’s always time to help cook lunch for the kids and spend some time with them playing and in the classroom. End the week enjoying some sea and sun fun and the beauty of the Dolphin Coast.
