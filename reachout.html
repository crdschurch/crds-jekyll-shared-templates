GO Local: Lead a project

During GO Local week, we go into our neighborhoods and do exactly what God calls us to do: serve others. We get our hands dirty cleaning up trash, planting gardens, making friends—turning love into practical, physical reality. It’s how the church shows the world what Jesus looks like. Sign up to be a project leader. You have the ability to lead and make a difference in your own neighborhood. Leading a GO Local project is NOT all weekend long (one day only) and is NOT a crazy huge commitment (approximately 10–12 over three months).
