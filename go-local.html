GO Local is when loving your neighbor gets practical

May 18-19, 2019

During GO Local week, we go into our neighborhoods and do exactly what God calls us to do: serve others. We get our hands dirty cleaning up trash, planting gardens, making friends—turning love into practical, physical reality. It’s how the church shows the world what Jesus looks like.
