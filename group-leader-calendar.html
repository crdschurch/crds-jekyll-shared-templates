Group Leader Calendar

Use this calendar to plan your group (and your life) well.

Stay in the know on leader trainings, and Crossroads experiences that impact your group. You'll see we even have some seasons for intentional rest built in. While you're planning, add a whole day OFF work (aka rest) every week too. We didn't make it up, so seriously give it a try. (It's amazing.)
