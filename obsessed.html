FAQs

What’s a Journey?

A journey is an annual five-week study for the entire Crossroads community, designed to help you go to a new place spiritually.

A journey has three elements:

  • Weekend message
  • Individual app work
  • Small group gatherings

All of these elements make the journey one of the most transformational things this community does.

What materials do I need?

Download the Crossroads Anywhere App from the Apple or Google Play stores. If you already have the app, make sure it’s up-to-date.
