Get in the Swing

This week we talk about rhythms. Specifically, how we work from rest. Yep, rest. And from there, we can tackle some huge problems. 

Key points

Working from rest is the only way to bear fruit. In fact, it's the first this we did after God created us.

Starting new things take discipline. But over time, that discipline becomes a habit, and eventually part of your lifestyle.

Try it out

Take the discipline you tried last week and turn that into an ongoing plan to beginning building it into a habit. Share that commitment with your Coach to keep you accountable. When you’re ready, go ahead to the next video.
